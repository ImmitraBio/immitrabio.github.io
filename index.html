Immitra Bio: Redefining Genetic Medicine

Immitra Bio is pioneering a new frontier in genetic medicine by harnessing the power of blood stem cells for in vivo therapeutic protein production. Our proprietary platform, HematoGen, enables precise gene integration into hematopoietic stem cells, offering a transformative, long-lasting solution for rare genetic disorders. By replacing conventional protein and enzyme replacement therapies, we aim to develop scalable, curative treatments that redefine patient care.
